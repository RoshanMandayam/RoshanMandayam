Are We There Yet?

A single player map-based guessing game.

Rules

1. Have Fun!

2. Your goal is to guess the amount of time it takes to WALK from one location to another (both locations are ANYWHERE in the U.S.A.)

3. Your score is based on the accuracy of your guess -- but if you overestimate, you get 0 points!!!

4. Don't forget Rule 1.
